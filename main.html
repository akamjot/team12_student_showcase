STUDENT FINALE PROJECT - BROTHERS IN ARMS
MEMORIAL

During World War I, over a million Indian soldiers served alongside Canadians in key battles like Ypres and Vimy Ridge. The Lahore Division's timely arrival in 1914 helped prevent German advances, allowing the Canadian forces to land in Europe.

The memorial will honor the bravery of Indian and Canadian soldiers who fought together. Over time, it will become a peaceful park with trails, benches, and displays, preserving their stories and sacrifices for future generations.
